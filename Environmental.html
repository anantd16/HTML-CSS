Critical Threats

  • Global Warming Rising temperatures disrupt weather patterns and ecosystems.
  • Plastic Pollution Microplastics are now found in the deepest oceans and our food.
  • Biodiversity Loss Species are going extinct 1,000x faster than natural rates.

Action Plan

  • Go Renewable Solar and wind energy must replace fossil fuels.
  • Reforestation Planting trees to absorb carbon and restore homes.
  • Reduce Waste Eliminate single-use plastics from daily life.
